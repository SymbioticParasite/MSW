Mushroom are going to save the World

How can mushrooms Save The World ?

There are mushrooms that can break down the neurotoxins used in nerve gas.
Sounds weird right?
But there's more .... Research show's that some mushroom species can clean up polluted soil.
The Ocean Blue Project did some extensive research into Oyster mushrooms and placed bags of inocculated woodchips at river banks.
As the mushrooms matured, they started to break down toxins and neutralized pollutants in the river.
There are many studies that show the potential of mushroom mycelium and fruit bodies.
